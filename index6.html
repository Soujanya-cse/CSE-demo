Computer Science

Computer science is the study of algorithms, data structures, and computational processes that enable the development and operation of software and hardware systems. It encompasses fields like artificial intelligence, data science, cybersecurity, and software engineering, driving technological innovation and problem-solving in diverse domains.

Information Science

Information science focuses on the collection, classification, storage, retrieval, and dissemination of information. It integrates principles from computer science, library science, and data management to optimize the use of information systems and technologies in supporting decision-making, communication, and knowledge management.
